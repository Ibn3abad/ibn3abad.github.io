# **Zakat Calculator**

#  Accurate, Private, Simple.

**Welcome to your reliable companion for Zakat calculation.** **Fulfilling your Zakat obligation should be simple, accurate, and secure. Our app provides you with an easy-to-use tool to calculate your Zakat based on your assets, helping you determine your charitable contribution with precision.**

**Your Privacy Matters.** **We believe that your financial situation is a private matter. That is why our app processes all calculations exclusively on your device. No financial data is sent to any external server. Your information stays with you, always.**

**Key Features:**

- **Precise Calculations:** **User-friendly interface for an accurate assessment.**

- **Privacy-First:** **No data collection on your financial assets.**

- **Completely Free:** **No hidden costs, simple and straightforward.**
